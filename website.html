Discover Paradise

The Iconic White Beach

Boracay’s crown jewel, White Beach, is world-renowned for its 4km stretch of fine, powdery sand that remains cool even under the midday sun. It is the center of the island’s dining and social scene.

Rustic Puka Shell Beach

Located on the northern tip of the island, Puka Beach offers a more rugged, natural experience. The sand here is mixed with small puka shells, and the waters are a deep, brilliant turquoise.

Bulabog Adventure

The eastern side of the island is the kitesurfing hub of Asia. Bulabog Beach provides the perfect wind conditions for water sports enthusiasts looking for an adrenaline rush.

Island Guide & Tips

Best Time to Visit

The dry season (Amihan) from November to April offers the calmest waters and clearest skies.

Getting Around

Electric trikes are the primary mode of transport. You can also rent bicycles or walk along the beach path.

Island Rules

Help preserve paradise: No smoking or drinking on the beach, and avoid using single-use plastics.

Currency

Philippine Peso (PHP) is the local currency. ATMs are widely available in D'Mall and major stations.
